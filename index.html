FAQ

Quick answers about me.

Do you need sponsorship?

Yes. I am currently on H-1B and would need employer sponsorship or H-1B transfer support.

Where are you based?

I am based in the Detroit, Michigan area.

What kind of engineer are you?

I am a systems-focused mechanical engineer working across hardware, controls, testing, data analysis, and real-world validation.

What kind of roles are you looking for?

I am interested in mechanical systems, controls, testing, micro-CHP, distributed energy, engine-generator systems, vehicle performance, motorsports engineering, and system integration.

What is your short-term goal?

To become physically, mentally, and financially ready for bi-weekly track days while building my C7 Corvette Grand Sport into my ultimate track machine.

What is your long-term goal?

To stay ready for track days for life and build a garage full of track machines developed through engineering, testing, and iteration.

What is your background?

I was born and raised in Shanghai, moved to Miami for school, completed my B.S. and M.S. in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Miami, then moved to Detroit to work at Enginuity Power Systems.

What project best represents you?

My C7 Corvette active-aero project, because it combines CAD, controls, CAN data, actuator feedback, testing, and performance development.

What motivates you?

Building real systems, testing them, learning from the data, and making the next version better.

What should people remember about you?

I am an engineer who builds, validates, and improves real systems.
